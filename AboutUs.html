Our Story

Welcome to EcoTreasures, where sustainability meets style. \nFounded in December 2023 in Ethiopia, our mission is to provide eco-friendly products that make a positive impact on the planet. From organic clothing to reusable household items, we curate a selection that aligns with our commitment to environmental responsibility.


Introduction:

EcoTreasures is an online marketplace dedicated to curating and selling eco-friendly and sustainable products. The platform aims to connect environmentally conscious consumers with a wide range of products that have minimal impact on the planet. From organic clothing to reusable household items and eco-friendly gadgets, EcoTreasures is the go-to destination for individuals who want to make sustainable choices in their everyday lives.

How we live and how we spend our money always carries a social and environmental impact, whether we choose to take notice of it or not. We believe choosing enviroment friendly products like solar powered products, organic cotton clothing and other organic products,EcoTreasures products has a positive impact on all these levels.
